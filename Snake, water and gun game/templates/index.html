Game Rules

"Snake, Water, and Gun" is a classical hand game based on three elements, each defeating one and falling to another.

🐍 Snake drinks 💧 Water

Snake wins against Water. Water is harmless to Snake, who easily consumes it.

💧 Water rusts 🔫 Gun

Water wins against Gun. Dropping a mechanical gun in water ruins its firing mechanism.

🔫 Gun shoots 🐍 Snake

Gun wins against Snake. The bullet stops the threat instantly from a safe distance.

If both choose the same weapon, it is a TIE and the battle is resolved in a draw.
